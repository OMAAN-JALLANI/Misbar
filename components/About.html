About Misbar

Excellence in Every Drop

Misbar Industrial Business Company is a leading refiner and supplier of high-purity hydrocarbon products, serving the industrial backbone of the Kingdom.

As a proud subsidiary of ChemRef Holdings, we are committed to Vision 2030 by localizing the manufacturing of essential industrial fluids. Our facility in Dammam utilizes advanced separation technologies to produce solvents, base oils, and process fluids that drive efficiency across sectors.

We combine technical expertise with operational agility to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring supply chain reliability and superior product quality.

Our Vision

To be the regional benchmark for high-quality hydrocarbon solutions, driving industrial self-sufficiency and sustainable growth in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Mission

To deliver superior refined products through operational excellence, strict quality control, and customer-centric innovation, while upholding the highest safety standards.
