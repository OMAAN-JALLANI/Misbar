About Misbar Industrial

Misbar Industrial Business Company refines and supplies base oils and wash oils used across the lubricant, metalworking, and chemical sectors.

Based in Dammam's 3rd Industrial City and part of ChemRef Holdings, we specialize in converting refinery feedstocks into high-purity hydrocarbon products. Our operations combine modern refining equipment and strict quality control to support Vision 2030.
